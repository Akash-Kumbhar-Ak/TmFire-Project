Oil project

An oil project typically involves the exploration, extraction, production, and distribution of crude oil. The process begins with exploration, where geophysical surveys and drilling tests are conducted to identify oil reserves. Once reserves are located, extraction methods such as drilling are employed to bring crude oil to the surface. The oil is then transported to refineries, where it undergoes refining to separate useful products like gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel. Finally, the refined/ oil products are distributed through pipelines, tanker ships, or trucks to consumers, including industries, power plants, and retail outlets, while ensuring environmental regulations and safety standards are met.
